The information Technology department equips students with essential skills in programming, networking, database management, and emerging technologies, preparing them for careers in the ever-evolving digital landscape.

The Education Department prepares future educators with the knowledge and teaching methodologies needed to inspire and nurture the next generation of learners.

The Accountancy department provides a strong foundation in financial accounting, auditing, and taxation, training students to excel as future Certified Public Accountants and financial experts.

The Criminology department focuses on law enforcement, criminal justice, and forensic science, molding graduates into competent professional dedicated to public safety and crime prevention.

The Business Administration department develops students leadership, entrepreneurial, and management skills, empowering then to thrive in the competitive business world.
